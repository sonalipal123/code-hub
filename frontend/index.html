Interview Questions & Answers

General HR Questions

  • Tell me about your background in coding.
  • Why are you interested in this developer role?
  • What are your key strengths as a programmer?
  • Where do you see yourself in the next few years?
  • What motivates you to write clean and efficient code?

Tip: Be confident and align your answers with the company’s tech culture.

Technical Questions

  • What is the difference between frontend and backend development?
  • Explain how databases and APIs work together.
  • How do you debug and test your code effectively?
  • Describe key principles of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP).
  • Which programming language do you prefer and why?

Tip: Mention real examples or mini-projects that showcase your coding skills.

Behavioral Questions

  • Describe a coding challenge you solved under pressure.
  • Tell me about a time you collaborated on a software project.
  • Share an instance where you optimized code performance.
  • How do you handle tight deadlines or bugs before release?
  • Talk about a project where you took the lead in development.

Tip: Use the STAR method — Situation, Task, Action, Result — for structured answers.
